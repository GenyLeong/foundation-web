GNOME Foundation Directory

Mailing Lists

Join the following mailing lists to hear GNOME Foundation announcements and to discuss GNOME Foundation issues:

  • foundation-announce@gnome.org (archives, subscription page) is for foundation-related announcements.
  • foundation-list@gnome.org (archives, subscription page) is for all foundation-related discussions, including discussions about candidacy statements. Minutes from board meetings are also posted here.

Email

To contact particular individuals or committees in the GNOME Foundation, email:

  • board@gnome.org for the GNOME Foundation Board of Directors.
  • director@gnome.org for the executive director of the GNOME Foundation.
  • membership-committee@gnome.org for issues relating to individual membership in the GNOME Foundation.
  • elections@gnome.org for issues relating to the elections for the Board of Directors, held each fall.
  • gnome-press-contact@gnome.org for the GNOME Foundation press contact.
